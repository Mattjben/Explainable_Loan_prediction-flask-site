Risk Mitigation Plans

If Data Janitor models predict a potential client is of high risk, the following Strategies can be used to mitigate some risk so the client can still be taken on with some strategies in place.

Perform Risk Based Pricing


  • Tailor interest rates based on high risk or low risk.

  • Give higher risk clients higher interest rate or cost of credit

  • In order to provide buffer for potential cash flow disruption and cost of recovering loans

  • Set lower credit limits and restrictions on amount that can be borrowed

    • Insert financial covenants


  • Manage high risk clients by agreeing to a debt covenant with high risk client.

  • Establishes predetermined agreement with client ensuring they stick to certain conditions with their finances.

  • Can include a set leverage ratio (cannot take on additional debt without increasing income).

  • If covenant is broken, creditor can take steps to recover funds and cancel debt facilities.

    • Periodic Financial Reporting


  • Request a high risk client send in financial statements or tax records as proof of income and expenses.

  • Agree for records to be sent in on a monthly, quarterly, semi-annual or annual basis.

  • Creditor will be able to see when a client may start having trouble paying off loans.

  • Will be able to take steps to recover funds and not be hit with unexpected cash flow issues.
